About me

Data-driven,
AI-curious.

I'm Siddhesh Sawant, an AI & Data Analyst based in Mumbai with a passion for building things that sit at the intersection of data and artificial intelligence.

My work spans the full stack of data — from cleaning and querying raw datasets in SQL to building live AI-powered applications in Python that real users can interact with. I believe the best analysis is one that doesn't stay in a spreadsheet.

Recently I've been exploring how Generative AI and prompt engineering can be embedded into practical products — like Healthify Clone, a personalised AI health assistant I built and deployed from scratch.

I'm always looking for the next interesting problem to solve with data.
